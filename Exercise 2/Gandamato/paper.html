Issue: Whether or not MinSuPala was already civilized prior to the coming of Spaniards.

 

            In the history of the Philippines, during the Pre-colonial period where the Spaniards haven't yet discovered the existence of the Philippine Archipelago. During that time the inhabitants of the Philippines are already civilized, in fact in the Southern Part of the Philippines: Mindanao, Sulu, Palawan "MinSuPala" are already civilized and ruled by many Kingdoms, Sultanates,  and Datu in different territories. During that time, the Muslims in the Sulu had already established their own government which is called the Sultanate System of Government. Pre-colonial Filipinos had already established their own different beliefs, cultures, traditions, and already managed in friendly trading or barter relationship towards the neighboring territories.

 

This habitual misconception keeps lingering up until now that the Philippines during the Spanish discovery is a country that had nothing but only ignorant and savage indios that needs to be civilized. But as we all know now that those statements are too far from the reality and based on many historical records which states that the Philippines had already advanced and already civilized before Spanish colonization. During that time, the region of Mindanao lies with the different inhabitants: the lumads and the Moros. The Mindanao is totally influenced by Islam where in up until now it stands as an image of Mindanao that it is directly connected  with Islam. The Muslims in the Mindanao had already formed their own government  called the Sultanate System and it was ruled by a sultan and it is undeniable to call it the most powerful government during that period of time. During that time, the Moro inhabitants had already managed to established Islamic Monarchy of Sulu and Mindanao which represents higher phase with regards to political and economic development than the barangay. The Muslims residing in the Southern part of the Philippines are believers of Islam which means they would completely pledge all their faith to the Will of Allah. Completely pledge all of their faith means that all daily affairs that will happen to a man will be on the authority or according to the will of Allah, be it government, educational, business, social life, cultures, arts, or whatever that may happen. The Five Pillars of Islam are the religious practices of Muslims, the following are the Five Pillars of Islam; First, Profession of Faith or called as shahada which means that There is no god but Allah and Prophet Muhammad the messenger of peace. Second, Prayer or called as salat which means to pray directly facing to Mecca for five times a day: dawn, noon, mid-afternoon, sunset and after dark. Third, Alms or called as zakat which means with regards to the Islamic law, Muslims should donate a fixed portion of their income to the community members who are in need. Fourth, Fasting or called as sawm means that during the Ninth month of the Islamic Calendar called as the month of Ramadan all healthy adult Muslims are oblige to abstain from drinks and food. Through that temporary deprivation they will recommence their awareness and gratitude for everything towards God that had provided them in their lives. In the period of Ramadan everyone will share the hunger and thirst to remind them of their religious duty and for them to be aware and help those who are less fortunate.  Lastly, Pilgrimage or called as hajj which means that for those Muslims who are healthy and financially enough are permitted to go and visit the holy city of Mecca in Saudi Arabia. In the Islamic tradition, Muslim women are required to wear a long sleeve that will cover most part of their body, hijab is suitable to wear for Muslim women. When it comes to food, Muslims are required to eat halal lawful foods which includes fruit, vegetables, and eggs. Also meat and any meat products that will be consumed later on should be from a halal slaughtered animal. Islamic Madrasa is a school, college, or a university that is often part of a mosque and has been established during the pre-colonial period in order to spread the teachings of Islam. Regardless of the Muslims inhabitants before the Spanish colonization, the lumads residing in the Mindanao had been already civilized way before the colonization period. The lumads or known as native are not a single ethnic group but rather they are those ethnic groups who are not Christians or Muslims. From the Manobo tribes to Subanon and all other ethnic tribes or groups are the first residents here in the Mindanao. Each one of them had their own different traditions and cultural beliefs, they even had their own tales, folklore and epics which had been told and passed up until now.  When it comes to musical heritage, most of the Mindanao lumad groups have a musical heritage that has been passed down by their ancestors consisting various types of Agung ensembles. Some lumads who lives within the southern high ranges in Mindanao are mostly farmers, they usually practice a little about trading. Most of them lives in group and usually ruled by a class of high ranking warriors often called or known as Magani or Bagani.

 

            The Mindanao Sulu Palawan or known as MinSuPala had already been civilized way back before the arrival of the Spaniards. Civilization doesnt only refer to how advanced the society in terms of houses, products but it is also characterized by the form of government, social structure, symbolic systems of communications like writing or talking with each other, and also its cultural and traditional beliefs. Firstly, the government has been established before the arrival of the Spaniards. The Moros had already managed to form their own government which is Sultanate System of Government which means that the ruler of a specific principality is a Sultan and has a sovereignty towards his territory and is being recognized by other leaders or Datu as an equal to them which leads to the forming of Alliance between the neighboring territories. Principality is what you called a place ruled by a sultanate. Because of the sovereignty of the Sultan, there had been a trade relationship between the Moros from the Sulu and the Chinese rulers during Tang and Ming Dynasty. Secondly, they managed to have their own language for communications towards one another, although Moros are the common rulers in Mindanao Sulu and Palawan but despite that the Lumads and Moros managed to have their own different cultural language that allowed them communicate with one another. Third, Moros had already managed to established their own school called Madrasah which is a Muslim school, or a college, institute that teaches all about Islam and the propagation of Islam towards the neighboring or Non Islamic territories during the Pre-colonial period. Fourth, the inhabitants had already managed or knew on how to about livelihood like farming, hunting for food and animal farming or industrial livestock. Fifth, every group or territories had managed to create their own weapons. During the Pre-colonial period, raiding or attacking a certain territory is a common thing during period of time, its like a survival of the fittest where the strong bear their fangs towards the weak and the weak or defeated tribes can only end up in two endings: first they may become slaves especially women and second, all of them might executed including the children. Spear, shield, and bow is a commonly made weapon by each territories during that time. For them to triumph their opponents or defend their territory from the raiders, it is a natural instinct for them to create and strengthen themselves using spears, bows, and shields. Sixth, every tribes or territory had their own different attires or garments that different or distinguished them from the rest, not only by their own garments or attires but also their food, some religious group like Islam doesnt eat pork but others eat them which distinguished Moros from the rest, and also they have their own believes, some groups are polytheistic which means that they believes in many different deities may it be animals, plants, trees, or even rocks, and there are those monotheistic religion like Islam which is to worship one and only one God and denying the other existences of other gods or deities.

 

            Mindanao, Sulu and Palawan (MinSuPala) had been already civilized before the arrival of the Spaniards. Its hard to imagine that MinSuPala is not civilized back then before the arrival of the Spaniards, would the Chinese rulers during the Tang and Ming Dynasty form a trade relationship between the Moros of Sulu if they were uncivilized. Chinese would not be so stupid enough for them to trade with savages, barbarians or a group of caveman. And the following statement mentioned above wouldnt exist or many historical records during that time wouldnt exist. The fact that inhabitants had already had their own different cultures and beliefs before the arrival of the Spaniards is already a proof that they are in the civilization process, they may not be as advanced as others in terms of livelihood, houses, weaponry, literatures and other staffs but they are already civilized.
